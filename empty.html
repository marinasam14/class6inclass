Class 6: Calculate the vote results for NY with Kamala Harris

Goal: Calculate voting percentage and determine which candidate won and by what margin. It should look like this:

Kamala Harris won New York with 56.4% of the vote, about 13 points more than Donald Trump, who had 43.6%.

Things to keep in mind

  • You'll need to format the numbers a bit -- there's a powerful tool called .ToFixed() in Javascript you can use to round numbers
  • You have all the data needed, but you need to figure out which variables to do and how to calculate it
